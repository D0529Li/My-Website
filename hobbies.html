Gaming

Gaming has been part of my life since I was a kid. I've been playing League of Legends for over 10 years now, starting as a casual player and eventually competing semi-professionally for my University of Waterloo team and other amateur organizations.

My competitive peak came in 2023 with some solid achievements: 1st place in East Conference, top 16 in College LoL Championship, and top 12 in the NACL Qualifier (tier 3 tournament of NA). After retiring from competitive LoL that year, I shifted back to playing casually with friends while still loving the strategic depth of the game.

Outside of League, I'm big on FPS games and single-player RPGs. I've put way too many hours into Overwatch, PUBG, Rainbow Six Siege, APEX Legends, and Destiny 2. My competitive drive has chilled out since retiring from competitive LoL, but I still get excited about discovering great new games.

Gaming will always be part of my life - it's one of my favorite ways to unwind, challenge myself, and connect with people. Whether I'm strategizing in a ranked match or exploring a new RPG world, there's always something exciting waiting to be discovered.
